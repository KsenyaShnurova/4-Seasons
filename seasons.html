4 Seasons

How to use the Artwork
The artwork shows 4 Seasons of the year. To switch between day and night times press "d"/"n" on your keyboard; To see and hear the falling snow click and hold on the "Winter season"; Hover over the "Spring" to hear birds and see flowers; Try clicking anywhere on the tree or ground in the "Summer"; To see the falling leafs hover over the "Autumn" and press the mouse on it for rain! Enjoy!
